WHAT IS SPECTO?

SPECTO is a privately funded organization dedicated to research and technological advancements in the field of digital observation and monitorization. Our main focus is development of the MDX (Monitorization and Data eXtraction) series of products, which are designed to observe and obtain data from a wide range of objects, phenomena, processes and even living beings. Our goal is to create devices which can extract data from any subject to learn more information about its nature.

The MDX series of products can not only read data from any matter, but also design its own User Interface on the fly to display the data in the most efficient way possible for the user in accorance to what is being processed. This means that the MDX can be used by anyone, on anything, without the need of training or previous experience, but does require general knowledge of how to use a computer.


OUR PRODUCTS

The MDX serves as the foundational platform from which all SPECTO devices are built. Specialized models are available for different applications and use cases, such as the DDP-12, designed for medical use, the ISM-9, to monitor industrial machinery, the AOS-14 for agricultural purposes, as well as many more.

Scalability. Future Capabilities.

As needs evolve, our products are designed to scale and adapt, ensuring they remain at the forefront of technological innovation.
While at this current moment, the MDX is focused on data extraction and monitorization, our head research and development team is working on implementing new capabilities. Our trial runs already show promising results in the field of matter transfiguration and data transmission into the observed objects. Bacteria and viruses have been successfully neutralized or even reprogrammed in this manner, although experiments have only been conducted on a microscale and within mice.
